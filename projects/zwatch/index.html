FEATURES

🕐
NTP TIME SYNC
Connects to WiFi on boot, syncs with pool.ntp.org and time.google.com. Auto-retries every hour. IST timezone pre-configured.
⏰
3 ALARMS
Three independent alarms with per-day scheduling (Mon–Sun), enable/disable toggle, and two-tone repeating buzzer pattern.
⏱️
STOPWATCH
Centisecond precision stopwatch. Keeps running in background when you exit. Indicator on watch face when active.
🍅
POMODORO TIMER
Configurable focus (1–60 min) and break (1–60 min) durations. Round counter, pause/resume, progress bar, flash transition on phase change.
🎮
4 GAMES
Breakout, Car Dodge, Flappy Bird and Pong — all with high-score persistence via NVS flash storage.
📺
CRT SLEEP ANIM
Authentic CRT power-off and wake animations. Configurable auto-sleep timeout (5–60 seconds). Display off saves power.
🔋
BATTERY MONITOR
Live voltage and % on watch face. Non-linear 18650 mapping across 3.2V–4.2V range. 10-sample ADC averaging for accuracy.
🔦
FLASHLIGHT
Full-screen OLED invert as torch. Adjustable strobe mode (50ms–500ms interval). Exit by pressing UP or DOWN.
⚙️
SETTINGS
Brightness (4 levels), display invert, 180° rotation, animation toggle, UI/alarm/hourly volume, sleep timeout, 12/24hr mode.
